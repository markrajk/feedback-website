1. GIVING FEEDBACK

The easiest way to give feedback

Giving feedback face to face is for many often difficult. This way it’s very easy and quick for everybody. Open the app, select a person and give a feedback with just a few clicks.

Create feedback routines

To increase the amount of given feedback team leaders can create feedback routines for example that everyone has to give at least three feedbacks before they can see their own feedback.

User specific feedback requests

Team leaders can also define user specific feedback requests for example that John will be requested to give Alam, Ella and Camille feedback twice per month.

2. FOLLOW OWN DEVELOPMENT

Everyone can now easily follow his own development

Everyone will get much more feedback than at the moment. Means everyone can easily follow how am I doing at the moment and how could I improve.

A lot of compliments and improvement ideas

Everyone will get a lot of comments. What went well and how to improve. Positive feedback makes people happy and constructing feedback develops.

Simple development statistics for everyone

Everyone can now easily follow his own development. The vertical bars show the average of the latest feedbacks, so basically how people are doing at the moment. The horizontal bars describe the longterm development.

3. TEAM LEADERS

Following employees development

Threw these new feedback routines a lot of great data is created. With this data team leaders can easily follow how their people are doing. Who is doing well and who maybe needs some support.

A great new datasource for team leaders

For example: What do Peter’s colleagues think about Peter at the moment? How do they see Peter’s productivity, how his team working? How does the trend of Peter’s development look like? What could be the reason for it?

Exporting data to PDF reports

All kinds of data can be exported as PDF reports. For example an overview of Peters six months development could be a good attachment when you as a boss talk with Peter about his development.

4. GIVING FEEDBACK

The easiest way to give feedback

Follow, analyze and compare

Team leaders can easily follow, analyze and compare how their teams are doing. For example in which teams people feel happy, productive and team work goes well and in which teams maybe not. And if not what could be the reason for it.

Requesting viewing rights

For each team the team admin can define a group of persons who have access to follow the teams development. For example Peter is leader of the sales team and in addition to that he has the rights to follow the developments of the teams of five stores.
